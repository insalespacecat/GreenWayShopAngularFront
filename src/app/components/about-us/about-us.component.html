The GreenWay Shop project

Author: Andrei Mazalo, student of Minsk Radioengineering College



Project's features:

For shop owners:

-CRUD operations on products;

-New orders tracking functionality for workers to provide

ability for them to call back for order confirmation;

For customers:

-Main page with products list;

-Cart management;

-Ability to place the order;

-Profile view and orders view;

-CRUD operations for profile info;

-Discount system for customers;

-About shop page;
